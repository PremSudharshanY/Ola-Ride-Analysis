## 📌 Overview
This project presents an **end-to-end data analysis** of Ola ride bookings.  
It focuses on understanding **customer behavior, driver performance, revenue trends, and cancellations** using real-world analytical tools.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze booking patterns and ride demand  
- Identify **peak booking time periods**  
- Understand **cancellation reasons**  
- Compare performance across **vehicle types**  
- Evaluate **customer & driver ratings**  
- Analyze **revenue distribution**

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🚗 Vehicle Performance
- **Prime SUV** has the highest driver rating ⭐ (4.39)
- **Prime Sedan** generates the highest revenue 💰
- **Auto & Mini** show consistent ride demand

---

### 💳 Payment Analysis
- **UPI & Cash dominate** the transactions  
- Digital payments are widely adopted but cash still plays a major role

---

### ❌ Cancellation Analysis
#### Customer-side reasons:
- 🔁 Change of plans  
- 🧭 Wrong address entered  
- ❄️ AC not working  

#### Driver-side reasons:
- 📍 Customer not reachable  
- 🚦 Traffic or operational issues  
- 🚗 Personal or vehicle-related problems  

👉 Cancellation rate (~22%) indicates a **major area for improvement**

---

### ⏰ Peak Time Analysis
- Ride demand is **consistent throughout the day**
- Slight spikes observed during:
  - 🌅 Morning commute hours  
  - 🌆 Evening peak hours  
- Stable demand indicates strong platform usage

---

### ⭐ Ratings Analysis
- Overall ratings are around **4.0 (Good performance)**
- Highest rated: **Prime SUV**
- Lowest rated: **Bike services**
- Indicates **better experience in premium rides**
